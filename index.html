How HemoBank Works

Donors register on the platform and get notified when blood is needed. Hospitals update blood stock levels and request specific blood types. AI instantly matches donors and hospitals for faster response times. Real-time dashboard for tracking availability and alerts.

An automated Blood Bank System that streamlines the blood donation process for donors and blood banks alike. Increases efficiency in matching blood donors with recipients. Provides real-time inventory updates for blood banks. AI-powered donor matching and automating emergency alerts.

This spectacular features of HemoBank are:

  • Donor Registration & Notifications
  • Encourages repeat donations.
  • Real-Time Blood Tracking
  • Ensures hospitals know available stock.
  • Use geolocation services to match donors with the nearest blood donation centers.
  • Emergency Alerts: Notifies donors and hospitals during shortages.
